Welcome to intimate objects, by Alec Molloy.

I love technology, media, philosophy, art, healthy living, education, and dancing.

For my wow statement I have made a hypertext document that communicates my identity through images, video, and text. I want you to have choice over how you get to know me, and I don't expect you to see everything I have made available here.

This medium is as much a part of the message as the message itself. I am a human being. I am complex. You have never met me before, but I hope you will feel like you know me. Maybe you want to get to know me better. The power is in your hands.
